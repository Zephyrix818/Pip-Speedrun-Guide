Introduction:

What is Pip?

Pip is a platforming game created on the PICO-8 cartridge by rarelikeaunicorn. PICO-8 is a platform for console games that run on Windows. Pip can be played here, and you can check out rarelikeaunicorn's itch.io page here.

How do you play Pip?

After clicking the first link above, you will be greeted by a PICO-8 loading screen. After waiting for a bit you will be greeted with a menu screen. Use arrow keys to move the selection cursor, z or x to select, and [enter] to enter the pause menu. Once you get comfortable with the controls, select 'Arcade Mode'. After a short cutscene where the falls from the nest, you will be in the game. Use arrow keys to move left and right, z to make a large jump, and x to make a short hop. The aim of the game is to collect feathers, and reach your nest, which is located high above. For each feather collected, you will one more jump, which can be performed in the air by pressing z. Some other controls including diving, which increases the speed that you fall, and can be activated by pressing x. A dive can be cancelled mid-air by pressing z, and this is called a dive-cancel. This move also uses one of your charges for jumping.
